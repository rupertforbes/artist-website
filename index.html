Rupert Forbes

Impressionist Painter


Hi there! Thank you for visiting my artist website. I am an Impressionist painter based in South-West London. I studied fine art all the way through school and have since taken classes in Oil Painting at Putney School of Art, while mostly pursuing painting as a hobby in my spare time.

I have a keen interest in natural beauty and as such most of my paintings fit within this natural theme, be it paintings of landscapes, animals, plants, or even (though less frequently) people.


I like to draw influence from a variety of artists. Some of the artists that have had the greatest influence over some of my artworks include Vincent van Gogh, Wassily Kandinsky, Salvador Dali and Richard Diebenkorn.
